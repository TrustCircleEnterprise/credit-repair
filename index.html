Laws & Resources
Know your rights — every law that protects you
Key federal laws
FCRA — Fair Credit Reporting Act
Right to dispute inaccurate info · 30-day investigation window · Negative items removed after 7 years
15 U.S.C. § 1681
FDCPA — Fair Debt Collection Practices Act
Collectors cannot harass you · Must send debt validation · Cannot call before 8am or after 9pm
15 U.S.C. § 1692
CFPB Medical Debt Rule (2025)
Medical debt under $500 removed from reports · Larger medical debts no longer used in lending decisions
New 2025
ECOA — Equal Credit Opportunity Act
Creditors cannot discriminate based on race, sex, age, religion, or national origin
15 U.S.C. § 1691
TILA — Truth in Lending Act
Right to clear disclosure of credit terms, APR, fees, and total cost before signing
15 U.S.C. § 1601
